Cryptocurrency Data Feed

True Price Discovery
Over 200 million trade and quote updates per day across 400+ crypto and fiat currency pairs.
Comprehensive Historic Data
Crosses are normalized and recorded with unique sequence numbers, allowing for retrospective analysis of market action.
Market Depth
Full level 2 order book data gives traders full-depth market by price by venue insight.
Extreme Fidelity
Backed by multiple redundancies and ICE’s SFTI Network, subscribers will never experience any down time, and will get immediate notifications of any technical interruptions at the venue level.
Translation of the New 24/7 Market
Daily summaries include a calculated accumulated volume, VWAP, turnover value, and open/high/low/last trades for each asset by venue, so you can close your books without ambiguity.

About Intercontinental Exchange

ICE is a leading provider of data and listings services, and operator of 6 clearing houses and 12 global exchanges, including the New York Stock Exchange. ICE Global Data Services (IDS) provides a combination of pricing and analytics, exchange and OTC data, data feeds and desktops, and connectivity solutions. IDS covers virtually all asset classes, from fixed income and equities to commodities and options, and is cultivating a new era of transparency in global cryptocurrency markets.
