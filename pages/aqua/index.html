Your bitcoin and Liquid assets,
all in one place.

AQUA supports Bitcoin and assets issued on the Liquid Network.
Buy bitcoin in two easy steps
Buy bitcoin and Liquid Bitcoin right from the AQUA app, no documents required. Multiple payment options are available including Apple Pay and debit cards.
Send payments faster
Transactions on the Liquid Network are fully settled in two minutes—no more waiting for those important transactions to confirm!
Keep your finances safe
The Liquid Network uses clever cryptography to keep your transaction data hidden on the Liquid sidechain. Not even Blockstream can see what Liquid assets are in your wallet.
Be your own bank, without the headaches.
You are in control
The keys to your assets are held on your device, ensuring that you’re always in control.
Your keys, your assets
Protect yourself from exchange hacks and thefts by keeping your assets on AQUA.
Easy backups
Make a backup of your wallet at any time by recording a simple recovery phrase.
Reimagine AQUA, your way.
Are you a developer and like the look of AQUA? The AQUA wallet is fully open-source, empowering you to quickly deploy a fully-functional wallet for the Liquid Network with your own branding and features. Stay up to date with the latest on GitHub.
